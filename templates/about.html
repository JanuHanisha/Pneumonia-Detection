About Hybrid Quantum

The rise of respiratory diseases such as Pneumonia, COVID-19, and Tuberculosis highlights the urgent need for advanced, accurate, and transparent diagnostic systems.

Hybrid Quantum is a novel hybrid deep learning framework designed for multi-class medical image classification and severity assessment of chest X-ray images. The system classifies images into four categories: Normal, Pneumonia, COVID-19, and Tuberculosis.

It combines Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for powerful spatial feature extraction with Quantum Support Vector Machine (QSVM) for final classification. The quantum component enhances decision boundaries and convergence speed compared to classical SVMs.

The framework also provides severity assessment through confidence scoring and integrates Explainable Quantum AI (XQAI) — including saliency maps, Q-CAM, and LRP — so clinicians can clearly understand and trust the model’s decisions.

Initial evaluations on diverse chest X-ray datasets demonstrate high classification accuracy, computational efficiency, and strong potential for real-time clinical decision support in healthcare.
